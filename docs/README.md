# Motivation Catalyst: User Help & Project Overview

Welcome to Motivation Catalyst! This document provides a high-level overview of the application, its business value, the complete user journey, and the outputs/artifacts generated by the platform.

---

## 1. Business Value Perspective

**Motivation Catalyst** is an innovative assessment and insights platform built on the principles of **Self-Determination Theory (SDT)**. SDT identifies three basic psychological needs that must be satisfied to foster well-being and health: **Autonomy**, **Competence**, and **Relatedness**.

### Core Objectives:
* **Enhance Workplace Dynamics:** By evaluating an individual's intrinsic motivation levels, the app helps identify areas where an employee (or co-founder) might be struggling or thriving.
* **Neurodivergent Inclusivity:** The application integrates specialized AI to generate tailored workplace and coaching tips for individuals with ADHD. This ensures that the advice provided is not just generic, but actionable and accommodating to neurodivergent work styles.
* **Bridge Communication Gaps:** It acts as a facilitator between employees and managers (or between co-founders), establishing mutual understanding and aligning goals for better productivity and job satisfaction.

---

## 2. The User Journey

The user journey is designed to be intuitive and engaging, guiding users from initial registration through deep psychological assessment and actionable advice.

### Step 1: Onboarding & Welcome (`WelcomeView`)
* Users land on the platform and receive a brief introduction to Self-Determination Theory.
* They learn about the value of understanding their core motivations and how it can solve common workplace pain points.
* Users enter their basic details (Name, Email) and context (e.g., Manager Name, Manager Email) to begin the assessment.

### Step 2: The Assessment (`AssessmentView`)
* Users answer a weighted questionnaire assessing their levels of Autonomy, Competence, and Relatedness.
* The application features built-in localization (English/Hebrew) and tracks question progress dynamically.

### Step 3: AI-Powered Analysis
* Once the assessment is complete, the application communicates securely with a Firebase Cloud Functions backend.
* Using the Google Gemini API, the system formulates deep psychological models matching the user's questionnaire scores.
* It generates distinct insights based on whether the user scored High, Mid, or At-Risk (Low) in the SDT categories.

### Step 4: Reviewing Results (`AnalysisView`)
* Users are presented with a rich, interactive summary of their motivation footprint.
* The UI displays a visual output (such as a **Polar Chart**) mapping the results across the three SDT axes.
* Users can view tailored AI coaching insights, specific actionable actions, and neurodivergent-friendly advice.

---

## 3. Outputs and Artifacts

Upon completing the journey, users obtain several valuable artifacts that can be exported, shared, or implemented immediately.

### Deep Analysis Report
A breakdown of the user's status within the three SDT domains. It highlights:
* **Autonomy:** Independence and self-direction.
* **Competence:** Mastery and effectiveness.
* **Relatedness:** Connection and belonging.

### Actionable AI & ADHD Tips
* **For Employees:** Direct strategies on how to approach tasks, communicate boundaries, or ask for help, customized with specialized ADHD tips.
* **For Managers (or Co-founders):** Guidance on how to best manage, support, and communicate with the user based on their specific motivational profile. 

### Conversation Starters
* Generated by the AI, these are practical scenarios or talking points designed to help individuals initiate difficult or productive conversations with their managers regarding their needs.

### Shareable Deliverables
* **Email Prep:** Users can easily copy their report, manager instructions, or automatically generate a pre-drafted email to initiate a transparent dialogue with leadership.
* **Visual Data:** The gap analysis visualization provides a quick, digestible artifact perfect for performance reviews or 1-on-1 meetings.
